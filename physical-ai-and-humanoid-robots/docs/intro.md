# Welcome to "Physical AI and Humanoid Robots"

This book is your comprehensive guide to understanding and building intelligent humanoid robots. We will explore the fascinating world where artificial intelligence meets physical embodiment, covering topics from foundational concepts to advanced simulation and control.

## What You Will Learn

- The core principles of Physical AI and embodied intelligence.
- How to use ROS 2 (Robot Operating System 2) for robotic control.
- Techniques for simulating humanoid robots in virtual environments like Gazebo and Unity.
- Implementing AI-driven navigation and perception using NVIDIA Isaac.
- Hands-on exercises to solidify your understanding and practical skills.

## Who This Book Is For

This book is designed for students, hobbyists, developers, and researchers interested in the cutting edge of robotics and AI. Whether you're new to the field or looking to expand your expertise, this guide will provide you with the knowledge and tools to create your own intelligent robotic systems.

Let's begin your journey into the world of Physical AI!
